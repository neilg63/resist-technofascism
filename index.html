Resist Technofascism

Coming soon to a jurisdiction near you.

Governments around the world are NOT rolling out tough Covid-19 containment measures to fight a deadly coronavirus, but to control the populace as we transition to a new econmic model marketed as the Great Reset. Its reality is no longer wild conjecture, but an active project of the World Economic Forum.. Its leading proponents envisage a bright new tomorrow of interconnected cities with attractive, intelligent and well-educated professionals collaborating from the luxury of their home offices on the development of a better, fairer and greener world, providing everyone with all life's necessities and universal basic income.

There's one big problem with this Garden of Eden scenario resembling a network of university campuses surrounded by high-tech eco-homes, serviced by artficially intelligent robots and interspersed with nature reserves. The majority of people alive today would be superfluous to the needs of emerging Brave New World.
