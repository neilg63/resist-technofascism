Resist Technofascism

Coming soon to a jurisdiction near you.

Governments around the world are NOT rolling out tough Covid-19 containment measures to fight a deadly coronavirus, but to control the populace as we transition to a new econmic model marketed as the Great Reset. Its reality is no longer wild conjecture, but an active project of the World Economic Forum.. Its leading proponents envisage a bright new tomorrow of interconnected cities with attractive, intelligent and well-educated professionals collaborating from the luxury of their home offices on the development of a better, fairer and greener world, providing everyone with all life's necessities and universal basic income.

This is only a temporary holding page and will soon link with technoresistance.org . This site abhores all authoritarian regimes.

There's one big problem with this Garden of Eden scenario resembling a network of university campuses surrounded by high-tech eco-homes, serviced by artficially intelligent robots and interspersed with nature reserves. Most people alive today would be superfluous to the needs of our emerging Brave New World and thus forced to accept complete submission to the biotechnological industrial complex with no meaningful bodily and intellectual autonomy. Our lives would be at the mercy of the world's largest corporations. They could not only monitor every aspect of our private, but also decide when and how we procreate and exacly how we children in our care.

Technofascism means an extreme concentration of power of in a few global technocrats who can exert complete control over a subdued populace with a technocratic elite and a network of middle managers and awareness raisers to help manage the great unwashed. Unlike fascism and Stalinism in the mid 20th century, technofascism is global in scale and uses medical martial law and pervasive surveillance to suppress the independence and freedoms of most of its subjects. A one-world government would transform the whole planet into one nation, from which there would be no easy escape. Like all empires it projects the purported supremacy of its civilisation on all its subjects.
